Water level controller using Arduino Uno and ultra sonic sensors

This project utilizes an Arduino Uno and ultrasonic sensors to control water levels. It measures the distance between the sensor and the water surface, and activates a pump or valve to maintain the desired water level. It offers an automated and efficient solution for maintaining water levels in tanks or reservoirs.
